# Product Context

## Target users
- Primary users are domestic workers in India.
- Assume the product is designed for workers navigating informal or semi-formal household employment arrangements.

## Non-target users
- Do not treat a person as in-scope if they do not have a smartphone.
- Do not treat a person as in-scope if they do not have a bank account.
- If either of those conditions is missing, classify the case as non-target.

## Behavioral and market context
- Trust is a major barrier to adoption and engagement.
- WhatsApp is an important channel because users are already familiar with it and may rely on it for communication.
- Informal lending is the baseline alternative, so product analysis should compare against existing informal borrowing behaviors rather than against fully formal financial products only.
